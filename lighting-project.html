designing a pendant light




This is a pendant light I designed and fabricated for my Industrial Design Projects final. Through this project I explored how materials and forms interact with light— the light shines through multiple layers of paper mache to produce a soft glow. Curled paper strips on the outer surface mimic the way long locks of hair hang and interact with each other.
