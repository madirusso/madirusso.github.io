making blind cabinets more accessible




This is a project from my freshman year. Our goal was to find a way to give a woman with osteoarthritis easier access to her blind corner cabinet. Her primary challenges with her current cabinetry were bending down to reach items and retrieving items from the back of the blind cabinet. My four-person team produced this sliding shelf system solution.

During this 10-week project, my primary roles were physical prototyping, shop work, and technical sketches.
