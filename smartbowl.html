smart bowl

Every dog owner knows the classic dog manipulation tactic: the dog convinces multiple family members that they are hungry and gets 3 meals at once! It can also be difficult for owners to remember how many hours have passed since their dog's last meal, especially for dogs with insatiable appetites.

The Smart Bowl can help the family keep track of Fido's nutrition. It uses a pressure sensor and changes in weight to detect when the amount of food in the bowl changes and records the time and date.
