designing a desk

I designed and fabricated this desk along with a process book in only 10 weeks. This is a floor-sitting desk with a drafting table mechanism, a drawer, and an inset cutting mat. It's made from red oak plywood with a danish oil finish.

The height of the desk is perfect for drawing and crafting while still allowing plenty of space for legs underneath. Its depth is enough to fully extend the legs under but not too much to prevent the user from reaching items on the far end. A drawer on the right adds extra storage while the open space on the left adds visual balance to the piece.
